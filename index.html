I am a multidisciplinary designer. I do UI/UX, data visualization, Product design, web design and a little bit of front-end development.

I think of design as a mode of problem solving. How can a I take a chunk of information and convert it into a form that can be consumed easily. I have been a part of a start-up, where taking up multiple roles and working as a team was important. I also like road-trips, campfires and music.

Interaction Design · UI/UX · Web Development

Hospital Discovery App

Designed in PYKIH

Objective was to create a hospital discovery, review and comparison tool. Challenge was, it could be used by users new in city and don't know their way around.

We had a map base tool as maps are one of the best ways to visualize location based data. A camera inspired focus area would help user focus their search within a specified distance. User also had a visual filter to for users to filter hospitals as per the specialities. Here is a small introduction video.

Interaction Design · UI/UX

T20 Cricket Scorecard

Designed in PYKIH

We re-imagined the contemporary cricket score card into a modern day visualization. This was made keeping in mind all the cricket fans who want to stay updated even when they are traveling or working in office. Our aim was we provide something that can help them consume lot of information in a very small time.

This was it, a quick glance score card. All the information you need in a single screen.
